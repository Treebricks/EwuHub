Instructions To Use Ewu Spirit on Mobile

  1. Without connecting to the ewuwifi you can not access any of the services. This means you can only access to this service if you are on East West University.
  2. Swipe right from the left edge of your mobile screen to see the options.
  3. Not every feature of Ewu Spirit work, but the options given here is still functional.

Faculty Evaluation

Here you can evaluate your instructors of every course you have taken.It's very helpful because most of the time ICS Lab PC is busy when you need this.

Class Schedule / Routine

You can check when will be your class, what is the room number etc.

Available Seat

You can check if there any available seat of a particular section you want to take. This feature will be very helpful in advising day and for add droping course.

Credit Information

You can check how many credit you completed. What is your current cgpa etc. This feature will not show the detail result.

Who is your Advisior?

You can check which faculty is your current advisior. Your advisior is your guardian in the university.

Advising Schedule

Here you can see which time you can advising on advising day. Remember this time is very important, you should not late to go your advisior on advising day.

Advising Sheet

Advising Sheet will help you to see all the courses and its section, class start and end time, lab time, room number of the class
