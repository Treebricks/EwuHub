Instructions To Use Ewu Spirit on Mobile

  1. Without connecting to the ewuwifi you can not access any of the services. This means you can only access to this service if you are on East West University.
  2. Swipe right from the left edge of your mobile screen to see the options.
  3. Not every feature of Ewu Spirit work, but the options given here is still functional.

Faculty Evaluation

Here you can evaluate your instructors of every course you have taken.

Class Schedule / Routine

You can check when will be your class, what is the room number etc.

Available Seat

You can check if there any available seat of a particular section you want to take.

Credit Information

You can check how many credit you completed. What is your current cgpa etc.

Who is your Advisior

You can check who is your current advisior for the advising.

Advising Schedule

Here you can see which time you can advising on advising day.
